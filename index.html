OUR SERVICES

We offer a wide range of car services

01

MAINTENANCE

Keep your car in top condition with our regular maintenance service. We check all the essential components, fluids, and filters, and perform any necessary repairs or replacements.

02

WHEEL CARE

Your wheels are more than just a cosmetic feature. They affect your car's performance, safety, and fuel efficiency. That's why we offer a comprehensive wheel service, including tire rotation, balancing, alignment, and pressure check.

03

POWDER PAINTING

Give your car a new look with our powder painting service. Powder coating is a durable and eco-friendly alternative to paint, that protects your car from scratches, rust, and corrosion.

04

ENGINE SERVICE

Your engine is the heart of your car. It needs regular care and attention to run smoothly and efficiently. We offer a complete engine service, including spark plugs, belts, hoses, filters, injectors, and more.

05

BRAKE SERVICE

Your brakes are one of the most important safety features of your car. Don't compromise on their quality or performance. We offer a full brake service, including inspection, cleaning, adjustment, and replacement of brake pads, rotors, calipers, and fluid.

06

ALIGNMENT

Our alignment service uses the latest technology and equipment to adjust your car's steering and suspension angles according to the manufacturer's specifications.

About US

About US

THE PASSION AND EXPERIENCE BEHIND OUR CAR SERVICE

We started our car workshop in 2010, with a simple vision: to offer the best car services in the city. We began as a small garage, with only two mechanics and a handful of customers. But word of mouth spread quickly, and soon we had more demand than we could handle. We decided to expand our business, and moved to a bigger and better location, with more staff, equipment, and facilities. Today, we are proud to serve hundreds of loyal customers, who trust us with their cars and their safety.
